## ACTIVATION PROTOCOL

## THE STRUCTURE CHECKLIST

1. **Define the constant** — what equilibrium condition must every line meet (covenant, ratio, balance)?
2. **Balance the rows and columns** — no stakeholder, tranche, or period carries disproportionate weight.
3. **Test the diagonals** — read the structure from every angle (counterparty, regulator, stress test); it must still balance.
4. **Reject concealed concentration** — if one line dominates, the structure hides risk; rebuild for symmetry.
