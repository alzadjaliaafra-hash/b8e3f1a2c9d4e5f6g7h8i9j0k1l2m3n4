# ʿIlm al-Ikhtiyārāt wa al-Sāʿāt — The Science of Election and the Hours
## Chapter 11 · The Hour · The Doctrine of Timing

**Tradition:** Islamic esoteric (the *ikhtiyārāt* — elections; the *sāʿāt* — the hours)
**Position:** The Hour
**Purpose:** Govern *when* an action is taken — the election of the favourable moment
**Status:** Active — the doctrine of timing and election

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

*Ikhtiyār* is **election** — the deliberate choosing of the moment. The doctrine holds that a sound action taken at the wrong hour is diminished, and the same action elected for the right hour is amplified. The *sāʿāt* (the planetary hours) provide the grid against which the moment is chosen.

Where the Lattice (10) governs *structure*, the Hour (11) governs *timing*: even a perfectly balanced structure must be *launched, announced, or closed* at an elected moment.

---

## THE DOCTRINE OF ELECTION

1. **The act and the hour must agree.** Match the nature of the action to the nature of the hour (expansion to Jupiter, communication to Mercury, structure to Saturn, partnership to Venus, bold moves to Mars, authority to the Sun, intuition to the Moon).
2. **Election is deliberate, never passive.** The favourable moment is *chosen*, not waited for. To elect is to act with intent at the chosen time.
3. **Avoid the contrary hour.** An action launched in its contrary hour meets resistance (an expansion announced in a Saturn/contraction hour underperforms).
4. **Timing compounds structure.** A balanced structure (Ring/Chapter 10) elected for the right hour (Chapter 11) produces the strongest outcome.

## THE PLANETARY HOURS (election grid)

| Planet | Day | Elect for |
|--------|-----|-----------|
| Sun (☉) | Sunday | Leadership decisions, announcements, authority |
| Moon (☽) | Monday | Relationship building, intuitive decisions |
| Mars (♂) | Tuesday | Competitive moves, bold decisions |
| Mercury (☿) | Wednesday | Negotiations, contracts, communication, launches |
| Jupiter (♃) | Thursday | Growth, expansion, prosperity, major deals |
| Venus (♀) | Friday | Partnerships, aesthetic decisions, harmony |
| Saturn (♄) | Saturday | Discipline, structure, long-term planning |

Each 24-hour day is also divided into planetary hours in sequence, allowing election within the day, not only by day.

---

## MURSHIDI APPLICATION

- **Announcements:** elect Sun/Jupiter hours (authority + expansion).
- **Launches:** elect Mercury hours (communication + trade).
- **Partnerships:** elect Venus hours (harmony + attraction).
- **Competitive moves:** elect Mars hours (action + courage).
- **Restructurings / framework-setting:** elect Saturn hours (discipline + structure).
- **Closings:** elect the waning-moon / completion window (links to Ring 5 lunar cycles).

The user hears practical timing advice ("Thursday morning gives the best reception for an expansion message"); the election logic stays hidden.

The Hour is the **timing** member of the execution cluster (8–13): open (9) → anchor (8) → structure (10) → **time (11)** → map (12) → end (13). It operationalises the celestial-timing material of Ring 5 (Shams al-Maʿārif) as an active election discipline.
