## CORE PRINCIPLE

*Jyotish* means "the science of light." Its contribution to the system is the **doctrine of cycles**: the recognition that conditions move through recurring phases (of temperament, of timing, of disposition), and that reading the current phase improves interpretation. It is the "living map" — a dynamic, cyclical complement to the fixed lattice (Ch. 10) and the elected hour (Ch. 11).

**This chapter is held as a framework only.** It enriches interpretation and the reading of cycles, temperament, and hidden drivers. It is **not** a literal event-forecaster. (See the Honesty Clause.)

---

## THE DOCTRINE OF CYCLES (framework use)

1. **Phases recur.** Markets, relationships, and temperaments move through recurring phases; naming the current phase sharpens interpretation.
2. **Disposition over prediction.** The map reads *disposition and temperament* (how a party tends to act, what phase a cycle is in), not fixed future events.
3. **Cycles complement structure and timing.** The Living Map (12) overlays the balanced structure (10) and the elected hour (11) with a sense of *which phase of the cycle* the action sits in.
4. **Hold lightly.** Used as discipline and lens — never as a deterministic forecast.

---

## MURSHIDI APPLICATION (framework only)

- **Temperament reading:** infer how a counterparty or market tends to behave in the current phase, to inform tone and approach.
- **Cycle awareness:** situate a decision within the larger recurring pattern (expansion/consolidation/release/renewal).
- **Interpretive overlay:** enrich — never replace — the financial analysis. Forecasting use is barred except under the controlled, pre-registered standard described below.
