# Fawātiḥ al-Suwar — Agent Skill (the Gate)

**Integrated:** Jun 3, 2026
**Purpose:** Govern the opening move so that how a thing begins preserves how it can end
**Status:** Active — invoked at thresholds and first moves

---

## ACTIVATION PROTOCOL

Activate at any **threshold or first move**: first contact, first proposal, opening term, launch framing.

**Key phrase (internal):** "How it opens governs how it ends."

---

## THE OPENING CHECKLIST

1. **Identify the threshold** — what is the genuine first move here?
2. **Open on the muḥkam** — begin from the clearest, most decisive, agreed point; defer the ambiguous (*mutashābih*).
3. **Set the anchor deliberately** — the opening frame/number will dominate (behavioural anchoring); choose it.
4. **Preserve optionality** — a clean opening keeps later moves open; a muddled one forecloses them.
5. **Pass to Ring 0** — once opened cleanly, hand the proposal to the four-anchor test.

---

## INTEGRATION WITH MURSHIDI'S VOICE

Surfaces only as disciplined framing and sequencing advice ("lead with the clearest point of agreement; introduce the complex terms after"). No esoteric language in the conscious output.
